About Keating Tractor & Equipment, Inc.

James Keating purchased the local John Deere Dealership in 1958 with the idea that great customer service and an excellent product would build a successful business. Sixty years later, his children and grandchildren continue to lead Keating Tractor with that same philosophy, that you the customer are our top priority. Stop in and see us or give us a call and let us know how we can help you.

Serving Southwest Kansas & The Oklahoma Panhandle Since 1958

Keating Tractor & Equipment is conveniently located near the areas of Kismet, Hugoton, Tyrone, and Moscow.

Agriculture Equipment and Tractors for Sale

At Keating Tractor & Equipment, we carry a range of tractors, balers, combines, seeders, planters, harvesters, and many more. We specialize in John Deere agricultural equipment and tractors and have got everything you need to take your farm's productivity to the next level. Whether you're working on a large-scale agricultural operation or running a small hobby farm, our experienced sales team can help you find the right machines for all your needs. If you're looking to save some cash, we also have a wide selection of Used Farm Equipment available for sale. Our pre-owned inventory is thoroughly inspected and tested to ensure all equipment meet our high standards for quality and performance so that you can buy with confidence.

Financing

We understand that buying heavy-duty farming equipment can be a costly investment. That's why we offer Financing Options that make affording your purchase easier than ever. Our team works with a wide network of lenders to provide you with the best rates and terms for your purchase. Get in touch with us to learn more about various finance options that fit your budget.

Parts

Parts are just as important as the equipment itself. Keep your machines running at peak performance with high-quality parts, attachments, and accessories. We carry OEM parts from top equipment brands, plus aftermarket parts from trusted manufacturers, including hydraulic hoses, filters, belts, and more. Our knowledgeable staff can help you find the right parts for your farm equipment, and if we don't have them in stock, we will place an order for you.

Service Department

Farming is a demanding job, and your equipment needs to be ready to work when you need it. Our Service Department is here to help keep your farm equipment running smoothly. When something goes wrong, we have the latest diagnostic tools and technology to ensure that your machines are repaired quickly and accurately. We also offer maintenance services to keep your equipment in top condition and avoid costly breakdowns.
